Spaghetti with Italian Meatballs


  • List of ingredients
  • Preparation
  • Recipe video

Prep time: 30 min
Cook time: 1 hour
Servings: 4


Click on the picture to watch a wine tasting video in Tuscany!


Ingredients

  1. Ground beef & pork mixed (500gr)
  2. Bread (4 slices)
  3. Eggs (2)
  4. Onion (1)
  5. Parsley
  6. Garlic (2 cloves)
  7. Italian crushed tomatoes (1 can)
  8. Olive oil (1-2 tablespoons)
  9. Tomato paste (1 tablespoon)
  10. Oregano
  11. Salt & pepper
  12. Basil
  13. Pecorino cheese (500gr)
  14. Spaghetti (1 pack)

Preparation

In a large saucepan saut√© the onion until brown; add garlic. Stir in the tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt & pepper; add water and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer for half an hour.

Soak the bread in water for 5 minutes. Transfer into a large bowl and add the beef, eggs, half the cheese, the basil, garlic, and parsley. Add salt and pepper. Mix well and shape into small meatballs.

In a large skillet, heat the oil over medium heat and brown the meatballs. Remove meatballs to drain on paper towel. Add meatballs to sauce and return to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes uncovered. Cook and drain spaghetti.

Ready to serve!
